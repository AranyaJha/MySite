Summer Internship

I was a 2 week summer intern in this Wildlife Hospital/NGO called PFA, Bangalore. I was part of the rescue and rehabilitation team and spent most of my time in the ICU caring, feeding and nurturing the urban wild animals. I used to deal with Macaques, squirrels, bats and many birds ranging from nestling to adults. I also gave a presentation regarding "Release Protocols to be followed for Wildlife".

Mountaineering

I enjoy trekking and camping quite a lot, so I went for two mountaineering courses from HMI. A 15-days Adventure Course in the 2014 and a 28-days Basic Course in 2015. Those were probably the best days of my life. The Adventure Course was had all the fun activities, but it was during my Basic-Course that I learned some really cool techniques about rock & ice climbing
